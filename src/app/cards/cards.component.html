Panda

The giant panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca) is a bear species native to China. It is known for its distinctive black and white coat and diet primarily consisting of bamboo.

Kangaroo

Kangaroos are marsupials from Australia known for their powerful hind legs, large feet, and strong tails used for balance and movement.

Giraffe

The giraffe (Giraffa camelopardalis) is the tallest living terrestrial animal, known for its long neck and legs, and found in Africa.

Cheetah

The cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus) is the fastest land animal, capable of reaching speeds up to 75 mph in short bursts while hunting.

Sloth

Sloths are tree-dwelling mammals known for their slow movement and spending most of their lives hanging upside down in trees.

Hippopotamus

The hippopotamus is a large, mostly herbivorous mammal native to sub-Saharan Africa, known for its semi-aquatic lifestyle.
